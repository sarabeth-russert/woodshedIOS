Common questions

Do I need a subscription?
No — Woodshed is a one-time download. No account, no server, no recurring fees.
Do my recordings sync across devices?
Yes. Your library syncs automatically across iPhone, iPad, and Mac with iCloud.
Can I export recordings?
Yes. Share any video via AirDrop, Messages, Mail, or save directly to your Camera Roll.
Is it okay to record at a jam?
A quick “mind if I grab that?” goes a long way. Most musicians in the old-time and bluegrass world are generous about it.
