What it does

🎬
Record at Jams

That lick somebody showed you in the parking lot. The break during a workshop. Whatever you want to remember — pull out your phone and catch it.

🏷
Tag What Matters

Tune name, player, who they learned it from, instrument, where you recorded it, and notes. Everything you’d want to know six months later.

🔍
Search Your Library

Search by tune, player, or instrument. Your recordings stay organized so you can find them without scrolling through hundreds of clips.

🐢
Slow It Down

Variable playback speed without changing pitch. Half speed, three-quarter speed — catch every note of the tricky bit without it going wobbly.

🔁
Loop the Hard Parts

Mark A, B, and C parts. Set loop points anywhere and let it run until you’ve got it under your fingers. Go woodshed.

📍
Chapter Marks

Drop a marker at any moment — the turnaround, the hard measure, the full-speed run-through. Jump back to exactly that spot every time.
